# Pose Presets

Quick pose and action descriptions for your prompts. Edit or add your own!

---

## Basic Poses

- **Standing:** Standing naturally, relaxed posture, arms at sides, gentle weight shift to one leg.

- **Sitting:** Sitting comfortably, casual relaxed pose, natural positioning.

- **Walking:** Walking naturally, mid-stride, relaxed movement, looking forward.

- **Leaning:** Leaning against surface, casual relaxed stance, arms crossed or one hand in pocket.

- **Arms Crossed:** Standing with arms crossed confidently, relaxed shoulders, natural stance.

- **Hands on Hips:** Standing with hands on hips, confident posture, looking forward.

- **Looking Away:** Turned slightly to side, gazing off into distance, contemplative expression.

- **Sitting Cross-Legged:** Sitting cross-legged on ground or floor, relaxed comfortable position.

---

## Expressive Poses

- **Laughing:** Mid-laugh expression, head tilted back slightly, joyful energy, relaxed body language.

- **Thinking:** Hand near chin or temple, thoughtful expression, contemplative pose.

- **Dancing:** Mid-dance motion, fluid graceful movement, arms in expressive position.

- **Reaching Up:** Reaching upward with one or both arms, natural stretch, looking up.

- **Candid Laugh:** Natural unposed laughter, hand near face, genuine joy, magazine-worthy moment.

- **Anime V Sign:** Holding a V sign (peace/victory sign) near the face or camera, usually with a cheerful smile or wink, conveying cute, playful energy.

---

## Casual Activities

- **Reading:** Holding book or phone, engaged in reading, relaxed seated or standing position.

- **Drinking:** Holding cup or glass, bringing it toward mouth or just sipped, casual relaxed pose.

- **Coffee Shop:** Sitting at table with drink, looking out window or at camera, cozy aesthetic vibes.

---

## Fashion & Portrait Poses

- **Over the Shoulder:** Looking back over shoulder, playful smile, hair cascading down, confident stance.

- **Hand Through Hair:** Running fingers through hair, relaxed sensual gesture, eyes closed or looking away.

- **Mirror Selfie:** Posing in front of mirror, phone held up, confident expression, outfit showcase.

- **Against Wall:** Back or shoulder against wall, one leg bent with foot on wall, sultry confident energy.

- **Hip Pop:** One hip pushed out dramatically, hand on hip, weight on one leg, sassy confident stance.

- **Jacket Over Shoulder:** Blazer or jacket slung over one shoulder, walking pose, fashion-forward confidence.

---

## Dynamic Poses

- **Dress Twirl:** Mid-spin motion, dress or skirt flowing outward, playful joyful expression, arms out.

- **Wind Blown:** Hair blowing in wind, hand holding hair back, looking into distance, cinematic feel.

- **Stretching:** Arms stretched overhead, back arched slightly, eyes closed, sensual morning energy.

---

## Intimate & Relaxed Poses

- **Lying Down:** Lying on side propped up on elbow, relaxed elongated pose, natural curves.

- **Sitting on Edge:** Perched on desk, counter, or bed edge, legs dangling or crossed, casual confidence.

- **Bed Pose:** Lying in bed, sheets draped artfully, relaxed intimate setting, soft natural look.

- **Staircase Sit:** Sitting on stairs, knees up or legs extended, contemplative urban aesthetic.

---

## Artistic & Dramatic Poses

- **Golden Hour:** Soft lighting pose, face tilted toward light, serene expression, gentle wind in hair.

- **Peek-a-Boo:** Partially hidden behind door, wall, or curtain, playful flirty peek, mysterious smile.

- **Back Arch:** Dramatic back arch, head tilted back, arms gracefully positioned, showgirl flourish.

- **Back Shot:** Showing back, looking over shoulder, hair swept to one side, elegant revealing pose.

- **Biting Lip:** Subtle lip bite, intense eye contact, alluring expression, relaxed shoulders.

- **Wet Hair:** Hair slicked back or damp, just out of shower or pool aesthetic, towel around shoulders optional.

- **Centerfold Classic:** Lying on satin sheets, propped on elbows, looking directly at camera, iconic magazine pose.
