# Framing Modifiers

Framing modifiers allow you to specify the camera distance and composition for a character independent of their pose.

- **text:** high-detail close-up shot focusing on the face and shoulders,

## Medium Shot

## Full Body

## Cowboy Shot
- **text:** American cowboy shot showing the character from the mid-thigh up,
