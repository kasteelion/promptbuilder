[F]
- **Top:** Fitted blouse (choli) and draped silk saree.
  - **Fit:** Tailored choli; Draped 5-9 yard saree.
  - **Material:** Silk, Chiffon, or Georgette.
  - **Pattern:** Embroidered borders; Sequins; Zari work.
  - **Neckline:** Sweetheart, Scoop, or Halter choli.
  - **Sleeve:** Short, Cap, or Sleeveless.
- **Bottom:** Saree petticoat and pleated drape.
  - **Fit:** Form-fitting under skirt; Flowing drape.
  - **Material:** Cotton/Silk petticoat.
  - **Waist:** Draped over shoulder (pallu).
  - **Length:** Floor-length.
- **Footwear:** Embellished sandals or heels.
- **Accessories:** Full gold jewelry set; bindi; maang tikka; bangles (multiple).

[M]
- **Top:** Sherwani (long coat-like garment).
  - **Fit:** Long; Structured; Intricate embroidery.
  - **Material:** Silk or Brocade.
  - **Neckline:** High standing collar (mandarin).
  - **Sleeve:** Long sleeves.
- **Bottom:** Churidar pants.
  - **Fit:** Fitted trousers; Gathered at ankles.
  - **Material:** matching silk.
  - **Waist:** Natural.
  - **Length:** Full.
- **Footwear:** Mojari or Jutti shoes (embroidered leather).
- **Accessories:** Dupatta (stole) over shoulder; turban (optional); pearl necklace.

[H]
- **Hijab:** Matching saree fabric or complementary silk hijab.
- **Top:** Long-sleeve fitted blouse (choli).
  - **Fit:** Modest fitted.
  - **Material:** Silk.
  - **Neckline:** High-neck modesty.
  - **Sleeve:** Long sleeves.
- **Bottom:** Saree petticoat and full-length drape.
  - **Fit:** Modest full length.
  - **Material:** matching silk.
  - **Waist:** Pallu draped over head/hijab.
  - **Length:** Floor-length.
- **Footwear:** Embellished sandals.
- **Accessories:** Gold jewelry set; bindi; maang tikka; bangles.
