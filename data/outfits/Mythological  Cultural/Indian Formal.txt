[F]
- **Top:** Saree (5-9 yards of draped silk, chiffon, or georgette fabric in vibrant colors - red, royal blue, emerald, fuchsia, or gold with embroidered borders, sequins, or zari work)
- **Bottom:** fitted blouse (choli) (short sleeves, sleeveless, or cap sleeves with matching or contrasting embellishments)
- **Footwear:** petticoat (underskirt in matching color); **embellished sandals or heels**.
- **Accessories:** Gold jewelry set (necklace, earrings, bangles - multiple on each wrist, anklets optional), bindi (forehead decoration), maang tikka (center-part hair jewelry), clutch or small purse, draped pallu (end piece) over shoulder.
- **Hair/Makeup:** Hair in elegant bun with jasmine flowers or loose waves with side part, dramatic kohl-lined eyes, defined brows, natural or red lip, henna designs on hands optional.

[M]
- **Top:** Sherwani (long coat-like garment in silk or brocade - cream, gold, red, or royal blue with intricate embroidery)
- **Bottom:** Churidar pants (fitted trousers gathered at ankle)
- **Footwear:** Mojari or Jutti shoes (embroidered leather).
- **Accessories:** Dupatta (stole) draped over shoulder, turban (optional), pearl necklace.
- **Hair/Makeup:** Well-groomed hair and facial hair, regal appearance.

[H]
- **Hijab:** Matching saree fabric or complementary silk hijab, draped elegantly (pallu can be draped over head or hijab)
- **Top:** Long-sleeve fitted blouse (choli) in matching or contrasting color
- **Bottom:** Saree petticoat (full length)
- **Footwear:** Embellished sandals
- **Accessories:** Gold jewelry set, bindi, maang tikka, bangles
- **Makeup:** Kohl-lined eyes, defined brows, natural or red lip, henna designs
