[F]
- **Top:** Traditional **silk kimono** (vibrant color with intricate nature motifs like cranes or blossoms)
- **Bottom:** obi (wide decorative sash knotted in a complex bow at the back)
- **Footwear:** tabi socks and **zori sandals**.
- **Accessories:** Ornate kanzashi (hair ornaments), decorative fan.
- **Hair/Makeup:** Traditional updo, porcelain-finish foundation, classic red lip.

[M]
- **Top:** Traditional **formal kimono** (silk in deep colors like navy, forest green, or black with subtle geometric or nature motifs)
- **Bottom:** hakama (wide-legged pleated trousers)
- **Footwear:** obi (decorative belt); **tabi socks** and **geta sandals**.
- **Accessories:** Decorative fan, ceremonial sword (optional).
- **Hair/Makeup:** Neat traditional styling, polished grooming.

[H]
- **Hijab:** Silk hijab in color matching kimono with Japanese-inspired elegant draping, ornate kanzashi pins attached
- **Top:** Long-sleeve traditional silk kimono (vibrant colors with nature motifs, modest high-neck wrap style)
- **Bottom:** Full-length coordinating under-robe or flowing skirt
- **Footwear:** Tabi socks and zori sandals
- **Accessories:** Obi (wide decorative sash), ornate kanzashi on hijab, decorative fan
- **Makeup:** Traditional updo aesthetic, porcelain foundation, red lip
