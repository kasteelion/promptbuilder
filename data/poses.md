## Basic Poses

- **Arms Crossed:** Standing with arms crossed confidently, relaxed shoulders, natural stance.
- **Hands on Hips:** Standing with hands on hips, confident posture, looking forward.
- **Leaning:** Leaning against surface, casual relaxed stance, arms crossed or one hand in pocket.
- **Looking Away:** Turned slightly to side, gazing off into distance, contemplative expression.
- **Sitting:** Sitting comfortably, casual relaxed pose, natural positioning.
- **Sitting Cross-Legged:** Sitting cross-legged on ground or floor, relaxed comfortable position.
- **Standing:** Standing naturally, relaxed posture, arms at sides, gentle weight shift to one leg.
- **Walking:** Walking naturally, mid-stride, relaxed movement, looking forward.
